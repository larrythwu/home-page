KungFu Master

Our Isaac Sim humanoid robot implementation features a physics-accurate model (1.34m tall) with carefully tuned joint stiffness/damping parameters across 20+ DOF. We utilize a PPO algorithm with a [400,200,100] MLP architecture for both actor and critic networks, training across 4,096 parallel environments.

Key modifications:

  • Custom Striking Reward - Prioritizes high-velocity arm movements while maintaining stability
  • Specialized Observations - Includes limb velocities, stance balance, and body rotation metrics
  • Training Parameters - 5e-4 learning rate, 0.99 discount factor, 32-step rollouts with 5 learning epochs
  • Termination Conditions - Episodes end at 16 seconds, height below 0.8m, or balance loss exceeding 0.3

The result is a humanoid that can perform dynamic kung fu striking techniques with proper balance and form, enabled by our reward-weighted combination of striking power and stability.
